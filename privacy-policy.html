Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

This policy explains what CS Course Atlas collects, why it is collected, and how it is protected when you use the website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile data you submit, such as username, email, and learning-goal preferences.
  • Learning activity data, such as module progress, quiz completion, and saved preferences.
  • Support request data, including message content, optional email, module/topic context, and timestamp.
  • Session and security data such as session tokens, hashed IP metadata, and basic request logs to protect accounts and prevent abuse.

2. How Data Is Used

  • To provide account login, saved progress, personalization, and synced settings.
  • To respond to support requests and troubleshoot user-reported issues.
  • To maintain service reliability, diagnose defects, and improve educational content quality.
  • To enforce security controls and protect the platform from misuse.

3. Data Sharing

  • CS Course Atlas does not sell personal data.
  • Data may be processed by hosting, database, and payment providers that are required to run the service.
  • Data may be disclosed if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.

4. Donations and Third-Party Payments

Donations are processed by third-party payment platforms. CS Course Atlas does not store full card numbers or bank credentials on this site.

5. Data Retention

Account, progress, and support records are retained as needed to operate the service and maintain continuity. You may request account-related data deletion through the Contact / Support page, subject to security and legal obligations.

6. Security

Reasonable technical controls are used to protect stored data, including authenticated sessions and transport protections. No internet-connected service can guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Choices

  • You can use the site without logging in, but synced account features may be limited.
  • You can update profile information from account settings when signed in.
  • You can request correction or deletion of account-related data through support.

8. Children

CS Course Atlas is intended for students and learners. If you believe data was submitted by a child in violation of applicable law, contact support to request review and removal.

9. Policy Updates

This page may be updated as features or legal requirements evolve. The latest revision date is posted at the top of this page.

For questions about this policy or your data, use the Contact / Support page.
