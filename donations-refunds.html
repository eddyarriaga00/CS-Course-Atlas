Donations and Refund Explanation

Last updated: March 11, 2026

CS Course Atlas donations are voluntary contributions used to support platform hosting, development, and educational content updates.

1. Donation Nature

  • Donations are optional and are not required to access core educational features.
  • A donation does not create a subscription, ownership interest, or guaranteed service entitlement.
  • Donation amounts shown on buttons are suggested support tiers.

2. Refund Policy

  • Because donations are voluntary contributions, they are generally non-refundable.
  • Refund requests may be reviewed for duplicate charges, unauthorized transactions, or clear payment-processing errors.
  • If you believe a charge was made in error, submit a support request within 14 days of the transaction date.

3. How to Request Review

  • Use the Contact / Support page and choose "Billing / Donation Question" in your message.
  • Include the transaction date, amount, and payment platform receipt ID (if available).
  • Requests are reviewed in good faith and handled as quickly as possible.

4. Payment Processor Role

Payments are handled by third-party providers (such as PayPal). Final settlement timing and certain dispute outcomes may depend on the payment processor's own policies.

5. Transparency

Donations help maintain infrastructure, improve module quality, and expand resources for students. Contributions are used to support the project and are not investments.

If this site later introduces paid products or subscriptions, this page will be updated with specific billing terms and refund windows.
